Kesatria UiTM

🪖

Komander Kesatria UiTM

Universiti Teknologi MARA (UiTM) Puncak Perdana

2025 – Present

Kesatria is a prestigious university-level uniformed body programme that trains students in discipline, leadership, patriotism, and teamwork. As a Komander, I hold a leadership position responsible for guiding, training, and commanding fellow Kesatria members during parades, training sessions, and national events.

Responsibilities include:

  • Commanding troops during formal events
  • Mentoring and coaching junior members
  • Planning and coordinating training activities
  • Representing UiTM Puncak Perdana at inter-campus and national Kesatria competitions
  • Upholding discipline, integrity, and the values of the Kesatria corps
